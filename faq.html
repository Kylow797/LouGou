Product & Care

Q: How do I take care of my shoes?
A: Mix warm water and a small amount of laundry detergent to create a slightly soapy mixture. Apply a small amount of the water/detergent mixture to a sponge, soft cloth or soft brush and clean the affected areas.Apply a small amount of warm water to another sponge or soft cloth to remove any excess detergent. Dry the shoes at room temperature. Please do not put the shoes close to a heater.

Q: How do I measure my size?
A: We recommend measuring with a measurement tape based on centimeters unit of measurement.
